Where to find the ingredients?

In the dishes on this website, you will find that most of the ingredients are easy to come by. However, in certain cases, the ingredients are quite rare. For example, galangal for the Tom Kha can be quite tricky to get ahold of.

The best places to look are supermarkets, but failing that you may need to find more specialised shops based around a particular cuisine. The dishes are definitely worth the effort though!
